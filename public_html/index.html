Details

Connect+ is your all-in-one transportation solution, designed to make your travel experience seamless and enjoyable. With our easy-to-use app, you can book rides, make secure payments, and track your trips—all from your smartphone. Experience the convenience and reliability of Connect+ by downloading our app today.

How it works

Step 1: Sign Up
Download the Connect+ app from the App Store or Google Play. Create your account by providing some basic information and verifying your identity.
Step 2: Book a Ride
Open the app, enter your destination, and choose from available ride options. You can see estimated fares and wait times before confirming your booking.
Step 3: Track Your Ride
Once your ride is confirmed, you can track your driver’s arrival in real-time. Get notified when your driver is nearby and ready to pick you up.
Step 4: Enjoy the Ride
Sit back and relax as our professional driver takes you to your destination. Rate your ride and provide feedback to help us improve our service.
Step 5: Pay Securely
Pay effortlessly through the app using Connect+ Pay. Our secure QR code payment system ensures quick and safe transactions. Keep track of your payment history within the app.
